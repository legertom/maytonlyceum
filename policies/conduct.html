Expectations

Students are expected to be respectful, responsible, and safe. Bullying, harassment, discrimination, and hate behavior are strictly prohibited.

Progressive Discipline

  • Restorative practices and interventions
  • Parent/guardian notification and conference
  • Loss of privileges, detention, or suspension when warranted

Prohibited Items & Substances

Weapons, drugs, alcohol, vaping devices, and tobacco products are prohibited on school property and at school events.

Digital Citizenship

Students must use technology responsibly and follow the Acceptable Use Policy.
