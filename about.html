About Mayton Lyceum School District

Our Mission

The Mayton Lyceum School District is committed to providing excellence in education for all students, preparing them to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every student achieves academic excellence, develops critical thinking skills, and cultivates a passion for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education
  • Integrity: We act with honesty and strong moral principles
  • Respect: We value diversity and treat everyone with dignity
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and approaches to learning
  • Community: We foster partnerships with families and the broader community

District Facts

  • Serving approximately 2,500 students K-12
  • Three schools: Elementary, Junior High, and High School
  • Over 200 dedicated staff members
  • Established in 1965
  • Accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges

Leadership

Dr. Robert Williams, Superintendent
Dr. Williams has served our district for over 15 years, bringing innovative educational practices and a commitment to student success.
