Application Process

To apply for positions with Mayton Lyceum School District:

  1. Submit an online application through our district portal
  2. Attach required documents (resume, credentials, transcripts)
  3. Complete a background check
  4. Interview with the hiring committee

Benefits

We offer competitive salaries and comprehensive benefits including:

  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • California State Teachers' Retirement System (CalSTRS) or CalPERS
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Sick leave and vacation time
